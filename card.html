Ferret

When playing they make tiny noises called "Dooks"

  • Life span: 8-10 years
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Activity: Needs at least 4 hours out of cage
  • Length: Some ferrets can get up to 45 centimeters long!

Ferrets are cute and cuddly little critters that will play all day and all night but when they need their beauty rest they get their beauty rest.
